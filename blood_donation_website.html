Blood Group Compatibility

Understand which blood types can donate to and receive from each other.

🩸
1.3 crore units

India needs approximately 1.3 crore units of blood annually, but only 1 crore units are available.

⏱️
Every 2 seconds

Someone in India needs blood every 2 seconds for surgeries, accidents, and medical treatments.

💉
450 ml per donation

One donation is typically 450 ml — your body replenishes this within 24–48 hours.

🕒
10–15 minutes

The actual donation process takes only 10–15 minutes. The entire visit is under an hour.

🌡️
42 days shelf life

Whole blood can be stored for up to 42 days. Platelets have only a 5-day shelf life.

🏥
3 lives per donation

Blood is separated into red cells, plasma, and platelets — each used for different patients.

✅ You CAN donate if you:
  • Are between 18–65 years old
  • Weigh more than 45 kg
  • Have haemoglobin ≥12.5 g/dL
  • Have not donated blood in the last 3 months
  • Are in general good health
❌ You CANNOT donate if you:
  • Have had any illness in the past 7 days
  • Are pregnant or have delivered in the last 6 months
  • Have HIV, Hepatitis B/C, or Malaria
  • Have taken antibiotics in the last 48 hours
  • Have a history of heart disease or cancer

About Us

India's Most Trusted Blood Donation Network

LifeDrop was founded in 2020 with one mission: eliminate preventable deaths caused by blood shortage. We connect donors and patients across every city in India, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

Our platform is free for everyone. We are supported by healthcare organizations, volunteers, and individual donors who believe that no one should die waiting for blood.
